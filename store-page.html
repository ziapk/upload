About Wild Things Funky Little Dress

Wild Things is an award winning label for little girls and boys which oﬀering simple, versatile clothes with a funky contemporary twist.

Little ones love to wear the strong colours and relate to the simple imagery in the prints, and grown ups like the subtle retro inﬂuences and elements of childhood nostalgia. Inﬂuenced by the simplicity of Scandinavian design, 60’s & 70’s styling, and random naïve elements and whimsical details which appeal to little ones, these little dresses and dungarees will make dressing a joy, whilst promoting role play. All items are proudly made in the UK, in small quantities, to a high quality , using beautiful fabrics and designed in our studio here in the heart of the Lancashire hills. Everything is fully washable at 30 degrees.

Wild Things Funky Little Dresses has grown from strength to strength with a global and celebrity following after launching in 2011,when designer Kirsty Hartley decided to revive her love for making, and a passion to create pieces with heart and soul following a career designing childrens wear and textiles for major retailers. Her ﬁrst craft book full of little things to sew is available Spring 2015, published by Orion Publishing.
